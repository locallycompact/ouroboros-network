(|&&|) ∷ TestableNoCatch prop ⇒ prop → prop → Property infixr 1 Source #

&&| is a replacement for .&&. that evaluates its arguments in parallel. |&&| does NOT label its result as an IO property, unless one of its arguments is--which .&&. does. This means that using .&&. inside an argument to conjoinPar limits parallelism, while |&&| does not.

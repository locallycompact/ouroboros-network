  • tvarUndo ∷ !(STRef s [a])

    A stack of undo values. This is only used while executing a transaction.

  • tvarBlocked ∷ !(STRef s ([ThreadId], Set ThreadId))

    Thread Ids of threads blocked on a read of this var. It is represented in reverse order of thread wakeup, without duplicates.

    To avoid duplicates efficiently, the operations rely on a copy of the thread Ids represented as a set.
